Srcorey.com

An interactive web experience that fully showcases the professional design work of Steven Corey.

Srcorey.com will be an interactive website that will focus on displaying the design potential of Steven Corey. The website will use multiple pages to display different design strategies to further the potential client base. The website will act as a portfolio to potential clients of the design capabilities of Steven Corey, while also being an online resume. In the future the website will look to incorporate websites designed for clients and their reviews of the work completed for them.
